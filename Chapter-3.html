We've declared war on time. We're creating a new world, without time. Time is not merciful. We are born, and our life is already trickling away like the sand in this hourglass.

The above are sentences said by Adam's character in Dark series - my #1 favorite series, explaining the philosophy of his organization which was based on time travel. This is the most memorable part for me from the whole series, because here where it clicked, that our whole world is revolving around time. This explains why I love photography, I believe it is the only tool we can use to trick time. Looking at those pictures & living the moment all over again. Thus, in parallel to what I have mentioned in the previous chapters, photography is an integral part of my life, freezing certain moments that means a lot to me. Below are photos - taken by me - of some of my favorite memories. 

Fish Market, Suez City, First trip with my family by the end of COVID quarantine, the market was empty, but it felt good that life is starting to go back to normal.

Oct. 2023, My Birthday Getaway by the beautiful sea of Marsa Alam. A self-healing trip.

My cousin's kids, in the countryside, where we celebrated Egyptian Easter together. 

I don't know what is between me & sunsets, but you will always find me & my camera chasing sunsets anywhere.

Zanzibar, 2021. It reminded me of how nature feels, something we miss because of the city life.

The Big Night, Old Cairo, Birth of Al-Hussein Celebration, in front of Al-Hussein Mosque. The first time I attend, 2017

Another sunset, from the beautiful city of Aswan.

A boy selling fool & falafel in Al-Moez Street, Old Cairo. My favorite place in Cairo. I paused & thought "80 years ago, who was looking from those windows?" then I took the photo.
