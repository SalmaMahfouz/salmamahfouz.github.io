Nineteen Ninety-Six,

in a small village in the countryside of Egypt, a girl was born. This girl is me, Salma. In this chapter, I will unravel some parts about myself. 

Early Life

Until I was 4 y/o, I was living in the countryside, this is my favorite part of my childhood because it was just me playing in the nature with whoever kid I find. Then we moved to Cairo, to start my education journey. I grew up surrounded & influenced by more women than men. The only man who had influence on me, was my father, who is always asking me one question whenever I come back from anything: "What did you learn?". My upbringing was always about learning.



Connecting the dots

Subconsciously I developed passion towards learning. This is why I chose to major in Engineering. It also comes from the fact that I love art, and Engineering is the art of creating things that would solve real world problems. The beauty of Engineering resonates in the fact that we have to learn about different fields. This brings us to my current focus, Cloud Computing.

Cloud Computing

is not only about studying the Technology itself, but it is about getting exposed to different fields to be able to design solutions using the Cloud services. This is why I love it. As a Cloud Support Engineer we meet many customers with different scenarios, even during studying I learn about numerous use cases that could be implemented using Cloud Computing.

Gateway

My childhood was the gateway of my current thinking & aspirations. Passion towards learning, accepting differences, creating positive impact, empowering & encouraging others, searching for messages behind art. This brings us to Chapter Two.
